Thinking of joining the 442nd SB?

Welcome to the 442nd Siege Battalion recuitment page. We at the 442nd are a tight-knit Arma 3 Semi-Serious Milsim unit set in the vast and immersive world of Star Wars! We're not just another group; we're a community of passionate players who bring our own unique spin to the Star Wars universe.

What makes us unique?

At the 442nd we pride ourselves on our close-knit environment and our commitment to providing an engaging experience for every member. We offer two OPS so that everyone has the opportunity to join in the fun. Whether you're joining solo or with friends, you have the flexibility to run with one of our following groups:

  • ARC
  • ARF
  • Mandalorian
  • Aviation

Diverse Opportunities Await!

Perform reconnaissance as an ARF trooper - finding targets and painting a picture of the battlefield.Bring honor and glory to the Republic as an ARC trooper and command the assault. As a member of aviation, take to the sky and provide air support with a variety of transport and assault aircraft. Does swearing an oath and joining the covenant sound appealing? Take the creed and join the Mandalorians!

The choice you make will help shape the battle and protect the galaxy!

When Do We Operate?

Our operations take place every Saturday (1st Platoon) and Sunday (2nd Platoon) at 7 PM EST, ensuring that everyone has a chance to participate without disrupting precious family time. Holidays are sacred, and we respect that, giving you the opportunity to spend those special moments with your loved ones, whether the holiday is on or near the weekend.

Beyond Operations!

Outside of our main operations, our community members join each other in games outside of Arma. You have a game that you enjoy, reach out to your fellow troopers and see who else plays.

Ready to Join the Adventure?

If you're ready to embark on an epic journey through the Star Wars universe and forge bonds that last a lifetime, join our Discord today! Our doors are always open, and our recruitment team is standing by to answer any questions you may have.
